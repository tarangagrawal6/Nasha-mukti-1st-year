About Us

Welcome to Nasha Mukti ! We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated towards people and human welfare

"Our mission is to provide a compassionate and comprehensive approach to addiction recovery, empowering individuals to reclaim their lives from the clutches of substance abuse."





"We envision a society where every person struggling with addiction has access to
effective treatment, support, and resources, fostering a community of healing and hope."

Values of our website

Compassion: "We approach each individual with empathy and understanding, recognizing the challenges they face in their journey to recovery."


Integrity: "We uphold the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and professionalism in all aspects of our services."


Empowerment: "We strive to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and support necessary to achieve and maintain a life free from addiction."

Commitment to Excellence:

"We are committed to providing evidence-based, personalized treatment programs delivered by a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about guiding individuals towards lasting recovery."

Hope and Transformation:

"Through our programs and support, we aim to instill hope and inspire positive transformations in the lives of individuals and their families affected by addiction."

Community Support:

"Our commitment extends beyond the treatment facility. We actively engage with the community to reduce stigma, raise awareness, and create a supportive environment for those on the path to recovery."
